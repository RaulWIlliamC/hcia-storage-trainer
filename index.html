What To Know

Core Ideas

RAID combines disks into a logical disk. It improves capacity, performance, and/or reliability using striping, mirroring, and parity.

Traditional RAID

Know RAID 0, 1, 3, 5, 6, 10, and 50. The exam likes failure tolerance, performance tradeoffs, and whether parity is dedicated or distributed.

RAID 2.0+

Huawei RAID 2.0+ virtualizes disks into chunks, CKGs, extents, and storage pools. Its big win is faster many-to-many reconstruction.

Other Huawei RAID

Dynamic RAID handles flash block failures efficiently. RAID-TP tolerates three disk failures and targets large-capacity disk risk.
